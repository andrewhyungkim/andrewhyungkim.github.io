NARUTO SHIPPUUDEN: PAIN & NARUTO

STICKER DESIGN

Pain, or Nagato, was one of the most unique and interesting characters of Naruto Shippuuden.
This made his battle with Naruto one of the most known story arcs of the anime.

For the sticker design, I chose the iconic eyes of the two characters as the main piece.
The style of the background was done on Photoshop to best fit the character's style or vibe.

Pain has a sort of otherworldly, but mixed with dark and holy aura.
Naruto has a wild, but hopeful and firey aura.
To achieve these looks, I designed a glitch art and a smudge art in Photoshop.

The text under the eyes are quotes from the respective characters, with the episode of its saying.
I chose what I thought to be the most iconic, but I put it in Japanese to respect the original anime,
but also leave the quote entirely up to the viewers who can't read Japanese, like myself.
