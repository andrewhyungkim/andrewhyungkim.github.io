SUPERGRAPHIC PROJECT: QR CODE RIVER

Supergraphics are large environmental graphics that usually run along walls and floors.
This work was done on a staircase at the Roski School of Art and Design at USC.

I had the idea of music and the act of sharing music with others through a QR Code, and used pieces of QR Code as symbols of music to portray it flowing through the staircase.
The QR Code on the outside of the staircase leads to Odd Future's Oldie, which I thought was very fitting because of the nature of the song.
A group of artists gathering for a photoshoot, but made a music video.
For artists like Odd Future and students at Roski, it's sometimes all about coming together to just make something cool.
