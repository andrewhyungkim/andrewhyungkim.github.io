DEAN - instagram VINYL RECORD DESIGN

Press PLAY before viewing. *volume on / not too high*

DEAN is one of my favorite musical artists.
He has been ever since he debuted in 2016.

DEAN released his song instagram in December of 2017.

FRONT SIDE


The song's narrative is quite sad.

It talks about how looking through Instagram,
seeing what others are doing or what they are accomplishing
makes us feel much more small and alone.

BACK SIDE


How we live in a world where information is everything,
but knowing more is makes us more fearful.

But I believe the song recognizes the reality
that trapped a lot of people,
like me.

A friend told me that when there is someone
that feels the same pain, loneliness as you do,
you don't feel so alone anymore.

I think that's why the song was so meaningful for me.

And I hope it does the same for many others.

I decided to design a vinyl record for the song,
one that I felt best represented the emotions conveyed through it.

But I also designed a sleeve for the record,
with lyrics of the song in Korean on one side and English on the other.

And I wrote a message in the middle;

A message I felt instagram was really trying to communicate.
