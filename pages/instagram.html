DEAN is one of my favorite musical artists and has been ever since he debuted.
He released instagram in Dec 2017. And I loved it.
The song's narrative is quite sad.
It talks about how looking through Instagram,
seeing what others are doing or what they are accomplishing
makes us feel much more small and alone.
How we live in a world where information is everything,
but knowing more is only getting more fearful.

But I believe it recognizes the reality that traps a lot of us.
that trapped me.

I wanted to design a vinyl record for the song, since I also love vinyl records,
but I added a sleeve for the record itself with lyrics from the song,
and the positive message I believe DEAN wanted us to think about.
Each side of the sleeve shows the same content, but in Korean and English.
I loved the song because it helped me free myself after I recognized the message
and I hope it can do the same for others.
