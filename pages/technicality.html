TECHNICALITY: PHOTOSHOP ART

Connections between mental health and technology
have been drawn for quite some time now.

Especially with social media, it is still unclear
as to whether it really is bad for your mental health.

Click Any to Enlarge

Each image represents one negative use of the internet
with a message for the users in a HTML 'p' tag.

Within each abstract color shape I created representing the Rorshach Test,
a symbol to represent the negative use was blended in.

The left refers to users who turn to Google for medical needs.
The middle refers to users who follow foolish and dangerous trends.
The right refers to users who compare themselves to others using social media.
