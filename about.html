Born in Seoul, South Korea.
Name is Andrew Kim.
Senior at the University of Southern California.
Majoring in Design and minoring in Web Technologies.


Starting at a small private studio in LA,
I have been learning design for almost 7 years now.

Although I've stepped into various types of design,
I have discovered a passion for positive design.

I'm interested in the power that design, as a voice, holds.
I wonder about what it can do for its audience, for everyone,
even if it's just for a single moment.
I want to know how my design can make someone feel.
